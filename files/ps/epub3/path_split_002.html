A PRAYER IS MIRACULOUSLY ANSWERED

The man of God was calling out to تيتراغراماتون one morning

to bring more people to the jami' (congregation).

He was on his face before تيتراغراماتون

and he was supplicating du'a (free-prayer) very loudly

alone in a tiny apartment.

This was the only masjid (place of prayer)

the people had for their meetings

because the godless leaders,

more sensitive to politics than to تيتراغراماتون ,

had thrown the congregation

out of a public meeting hall.

But praise be to God who hears and answers

when we call on Him!

There is none like the Lord our God,

praise be unto Him.

He says somewhere,

"Before they call I will answer,

and while they are yet speaking I will hear."

In fact, the request of the man of God

had been answered weeks before he made his petition.
